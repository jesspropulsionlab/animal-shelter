Our featured pets!

This is Puggington.

He is a pug, who enjoys spending his free time dressed as a unicorn. He's a unipug. He's a pugicorn. Whatever he is, he's fabulous. He likes long walks, but can't breathe well enough to go on them. He likes rainy weather, but hates being wet. He also loves human food, but is on a strict canned dog food diet. He loves children, but can't stand babies. He smells all the time, and has terrible gas. He also enjoys being pushed around in a stroller. What a catch!

This is Cornwallis.

Cornwallis has a lot of energy. He probably would love nothing more to go hunting all day, and bring back lots of beautiful waterfoal for his owners. Alas, we'll never know for sure, because Cornwallis is deathly allergic to birds. He doesn't like children, and seems to hate older women, but would do really well in a house with a couple of men, and no other pets. Definitely no birds. He is very cuddly, but also sheds like a sheep, if sheep shed. He is very soft, so there's that.

This is Georgia.

Georgia is the happiest dog we've ever had in our shelter. She smiles constantly. We've never heard he make sad sounds, or even look sad. When you are around her, you can't be sad either. It's literally impossible. She loves everyone and everything, and she's perfect. When I see her, my heart feels lighter. All of my stomach issues, and heart palpatations just float away. She is a purr-fect princess. On second thought... maybe she is not available.

This is Remus.

Remus is a baby. Literally, he is a kitten. But more than that, he seems very young, and naive. He's probably never had anything bad happen to him yet, that's probably why. Just wait, young Remus. Soon, you'll grow up and realize that everything is terrible. People will judge you, because you're sort of a redhead cat. They won't want you teaching their children, and eventually you'll have a son named Teddy who you will wind up orphaning, due to your good-heartedness, despite the difficulties you've endured in your life.

This is Jefe

Jefe is in charge. He's a no nonsense kind of cat. He hates everyone. He's basically the exact opposite of Georgia. He always seems mad. No idea about what, though. He always looks grumpy. He seems incapable of making any face other than sourpuss. But for all of his negativity, he always has a soothing presence. He'll stay in a room with you, even though it's pretty obvious he'd rather be alone. Sometimes he'll let out a tiny meow that let's you know that even though he hates you, he's still got your back.

This is Patricia

Patricia was named by a small child, and we know it isn't a great name for a cat, but we didn't have the heart to tell her it sucked. Patricia is a pretty average cat. She likes her alone time, but she'll also lay near you on the couch. She has never been outside, so she doesn't know what she's missing in that regard. She does use a litter box, but she isn't great at covering her turds up. Maybe no one ever taught her how. She's terrible when it comes to knocking things off of tables, but she usually looks sorry after she does it.
